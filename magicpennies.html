Magic Pennies



Aspects of distributed ledgers (a category of tech that includes blockchain) can be hard to grasp. We made “Magic Pennies” to simply convey one of the most critical: how data stored outside the firewall can be strongly secured. Enjoy. :)

More to Come...

The scope of “Magic Pennies” does not include the basic structure of a distributed ledger or how it can be immutable, important concepts we may cover in future videos.

One tricky issue Magic Pennies does introduce, but not resolve, is key management. Some users will lose their keys or have them stolen, and must be able to recover control of their accounts without the assistance of a central authority. With Sovrin there is no central authority, and even if there were, storing backups “in the castle” cannot be the answer as it re-creates the “honeypot” we’re trying to avoid in the first place.

Stay tuned for some creative and exciting advancements that address key management, to be announced soon.
